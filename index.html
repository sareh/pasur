SAREH'S PASUR

Welcome to Pasur! Pick a card from your hand and then pick one from the pool, to get to 11! If you do, you win those cards.

Kings & Queens can only pick up their own pictured card.

Jacks picks up all number cards, plus other Jacks!

But don't let the main pool get too packed, or else it'll be game over for you.

Good luck!
